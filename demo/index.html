edX Console Logos playground. Hit Ctrl/CMD + Shift/Opt + J and check it out.

Examples

edX Logos

console.edx_logo();
console.edx_logo({'type':'image'});
console.edx_logo({'type':'css'});
console.edx_logo({'type':'smalltext'});
console.edx_logo({'type':'bigtext'});

OPEN edX Logos

console.open_edx_logo();
console.open_edx_logo({'type':'image'});
console.open_edx_logo({'type':'css'});
console.open_edx_logo({'type':'smalltext'});
console.open_edx_logo({'type':'bigtext'});
